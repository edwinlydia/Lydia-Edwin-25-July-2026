Guest Information

Everything you need to join us for our wedding day — from where to stay, to how to get to the reception with ease.

Dress Code - Formal

Ladies – Dresses or elegant formalwear.

Hats and fascinators warmly welcomed.

Gentlemen - Suits or smart tailoring.

Parking

There is parking available at both venues:

  • Olton Friary — On‑site parking is available behind the church. Cars may also be left at Olton and West Warwickshire Sports Complex around the corner from the Friary, where we may wet the whistle before the ceremony.
  • Blyth Hall — Plenty of parking on the grounds.

Accommodation

  • The White Lion, Coleshill — A traditional coaching inn with comfortable rooms and a friendly atmosphere. Just a short drive from Blyth Hall, making it one of the most convenient options for guests wanting something close and characterful. We have booked a limited number of rooms at this pub. It is located approximately 15 minutes from the Church and 15 minutes from the reception. The hotel is mostly full of wedding guests. Please contact us if you would like a room here.
  • The Belfry Hotel & Resort — A well-known luxury resort with spacious rooms, multiple restaurants, and a spa. Around 10–12 minutes from Blyth Hall, ideal for guests who want a more indulgent stay with plenty of facilities. Suitable for anyone looking to sneak in a round before the wedding.
  • Resorts World Birmingham — A modern hotel complex next to the NEC, offering a wide choice of restaurants, bars, and entertainment. Approximately 15 minutes away and perfect for guests who prefer a lively setting with lots to do.
  • Coleshill Pubs & Inns — Coleshill has several welcoming pubs and small hotels just 5–7 minutes from Blyth Hall, offering simple, comfortable rooms in the heart of the town. Great for guests who want convenience and easy access.

Travel

Both venues are easy to reach by car or taxi. Below is everything you need to get between the church and the reception smoothly.

By Car

  • Olton Friary RC Church — B92 7BL
  • Blyth Hall — B46 2AF

Taxi Options

  • A2B Radio Cars — Reliable and local.
  • Uber — Widely available in the area.
  • Solihull Taxis — Good for larger groups.

Train

The nearest stations are Olton (for the church) and Coleshill Parkway (for Blyth Hall). Please note these two stations are not on the same line!
