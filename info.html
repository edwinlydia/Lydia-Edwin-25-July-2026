Guest Information

Everything you need to join us for our wedding day — from where to stay, to how to get to the reception with ease.

Accommodation

If you're staying overnight, here are a few places close to Blyth Hall. We’ve chosen options that are convenient, comfortable, and make the journey to the reception wonderfully simple.

  • Forest Hotel, Dorridge — A boutique hotel with a warm atmosphere and excellent food. Just a few minutes from Blyth Hall and ideal for guests who want something stylish and close by.
  • Hogarths Hotel, Solihull — Set in beautiful grounds and perfect for a relaxed stay. Around 10–12 minutes from the reception, with plenty of parking and a peaceful setting.
  • Premier Inn, Solihull (Hockley Heath) — A simple, reliable option very close to Blyth Hall. Great for guests who want convenience and easy access.
  • Village Hotel, Solihull — Modern, practical, and about 15 minutes away. Good facilities and easy access to major roads.

Travel

Both venues are easy to reach by car or taxi. Below is everything you need to get between the church and the reception smoothly.

By Car

  • Olton Friary RC Church — B92 7BL
  • Blyth Hall — B46 2AF

Taxi Options

  • A2B Radio Cars — Reliable and local.
  • Uber — Widely available in the area.
  • Solihull Taxis — Good for larger groups.

Train

The nearest stations are Olton (for the church) and Coleshill PArkway (for Blyth Hall). Please note these two stations are not on the same line!

Parking

There is parking available at both venues:

  • Olton Friary — On‑site parking is available behind the church. Cars may also be left at Olton and West Warwickshire Sports Complex around the corner from the Friary, where we may wet the whistle before the ceremony.
  • Blyth Hall — Plenty of parking on the grounds.
